### Navigating Eye Mask Use and Inward Focus

**Jason Foster:**
Also, *it’s okay to come up for air*.

If they take the eye mask off, you might ask:

> “What was it like for you to be inward?”

Just a little validation might give them the courage to go back in. For example:

> “That was scary for you. Do you want more of that?”

**Jason Foster:**
No matter how much we emphasize that they can remove the eye mask, some people will lie there *petrified*, thinking they’re supposed to keep it on.

They don’t want to do it wrong.

So this is where *attunement and checking in* come in. You can gently remind them:

> “It’s okay to take the mask off if that’s what feels right.”
