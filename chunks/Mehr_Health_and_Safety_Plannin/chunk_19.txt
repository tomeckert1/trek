### Relative Contraindications (Caution / Case-by-Case)

**Dr. Seth Mehr:**
These conditions require careful screening, potential medical consultation, and individualized safety planning:
- Bipolar II disorder
- Borderline personality disorder
- Dissociative disorders
- Severe PTSD
- Substance use disorder
- Recent withdrawal from alcohol, benzodiazepines, or opioids
- Traumatic brain injury (TBI)
- Unstable diabetes (requiring insulin management)
- Severe mobility limitations (fall risks)
- Spinal cord injuries
- Chronic aneurysms (brain or aorta)
- Pregnancy and lactation
- Severe hypertension
- History of HPPD (Hallucinogen Persisting Perception Disorder)
