### Absolute Contraindications (No-Go)

**Dr. Seth Mehr:**
These conditions *should* automatically disqualify a client from receiving psilocybin services:

- Current use of Lithium (within the last 30 days)
- Active suicidal ideation with intent/plan
- History of psychosis
- Mania or bipolar I disorder with history of manic episodes
- Schizophrenia or schizoaffective disorder
- Long QTc interval (heart electrical conduction abnormality)
- Severe cardiac disease
- Uncontrolled seizure disorder
- Severe liver disease
- Uncontrolled angle-closure glaucoma
- Severe cognitive impairment affecting consent
