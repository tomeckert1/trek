### Psychosis and Mania

**Dr. Seth Mehr:**
*Risks:*
- Psilocybin may precipitate:
  - *Psychosis* (loss of contact with reality)
  - *Mania* (excessive euphoria, impulsivity)

*Higher Risk Clients:*
- History of:
  - Schizophrenia
  - Bipolar disorder
  - Past psychosis
- *Family history* of psychosis or mania (parent, sibling, child).

*Action:*
Push pause, gather detailed history, consult, and possibly decline services or add intensive safety planning.
