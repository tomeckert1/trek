### Psilocybin for Smoking Cessation

**Dr. Erica Zelfand:**
- Participants: 15 smokers (avg. age 51, smoking 1 pack/day for 30+ years)
- Intervention: Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) + 3 psilocybin sessions
- Outcomes:  
  - 80% quit at 6 months  
  - 67% still smoke-free at 12 months  

*Study Overview:*
  - Higher likelihood of success linked to having a mystical experience

> “86% rated the psilocybin experience among the top five most meaningful or spiritual experiences of their lives.”

*Significance:*
First NIH-funded psilocybin study in 50 years.
