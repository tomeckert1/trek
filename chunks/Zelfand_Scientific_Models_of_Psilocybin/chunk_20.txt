### The Role of Awe

**Dr. Erica Zelfand:**
*Definition:*
> An overwhelming feeling of reverence, admiration, or fear caused by something grand, sublime, or powerful.

*Effects of Awe:*
- Improved wellbeing and life satisfaction
- Increased empathy and social connection
- Reduced mental distress and negativity
- Decreased activity in the default mode network

*Ways to Elicit Awe:*
- Religion or spirituality (if aligned)
- Art, music, inspiring books or movies
- Nature
- Psychedelics
- Virtual reality
