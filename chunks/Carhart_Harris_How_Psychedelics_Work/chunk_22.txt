### REBUS: Relaxed Beliefs Under Psychedelics

**Robin Carhart-Harris:**
- Psychedelics are hypothesized to *de-weight internal predictive models*
- These “relaxed beliefs” allow for reprocessing of entrenched thought and behavior patterns
- Related to models like *canalization* and *default mode network disruption*

> “Under psychedelics, your top-down models relax, allowing new bottom-up information to come in.”
