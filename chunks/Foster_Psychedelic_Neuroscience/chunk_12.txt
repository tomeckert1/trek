### Psychedelics Unlock the Underworld

**Jason Foster:**
Psychedelics:
- *Inhibit the Default Mode Network (DMN)*
- Relax the *reducing valve*
- Reduce the function of the *superego*
- Allow access to *forbidden emotions* and *traumatic memories*

### Role of the Prefrontal Cortex
- *Reviews and contextualizes* the emerging material
- Reduces *limbic reactivity*
- Supports *reframing* and long-term integration

> Schemas begin to shift.

> - “This happened in the past, and I’m safe now.”  
> - “I’m strong enough to protect myself now.”  
> - “I am not what happened to me.”
