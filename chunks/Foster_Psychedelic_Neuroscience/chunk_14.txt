### Quiet Journeys Can Be Healing Too

**Jason Foster:**
Not all underworld journeys look dramatic:

*Examples of cathartic processes:*
- Some involve *quiet narration* of trauma
- Others include *dancing*, *laughing*, or gentle movement

These are also expressions of *frozen energy thawing*—the body moving out of freeze.

### When the DMN Doesn't Fully Go Offline

**Jason Foster:**
Some clients remain *locked in an internal struggle*:
- The *default mode network* doesn’t fully release
- The *fascist superego* is still *fighting for control*
- Clients may resist letting go of suppression strategies

*Presentations:*
- Images of abusers
- Feelings of shame, fear, or confusion
- Physical contraction (e.g. hunched posture)
